===============================================
HOW TO ADD REAL SOUND EFFECTS TO YOUR COIN TOSS APP
===============================================

The app is set up to play sounds, but you need to add real MP3 files!

STEP 1: GET FREE SOUND EFFECTS
-------------------------------
Visit these websites for free sounds:

1. Freesound.org - https://freesound.org/
   - Search for "coin flip" or "coin spin"
   - Download MP3 format

2. Mixkit - https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
   - Browse "Money" or "Metal" categories
   - Download directly

3. Zapsplat - https://www.zapsplat.com/
   - Search "coin" sounds
   - Free with attribution

STEP 2: PREPARE YOUR SOUNDS
----------------------------
You need TWO sound files:

1. COIN FLIP sound (when coin starts spinning)
   - Look for: whoosh, swoosh, spinning sounds
   - Duration: 1-2 seconds
   - Example searches: "coin flip", "metal spin", "whoosh"

2. COIN LAND sound (when coin hits surface)
   - Look for: clink, ding, impact sounds  
   - Duration: 0.5-1 second
   - Example searches: "coin drop", "metal clink", "ding"

STEP 3: RENAME FILES
--------------------
Rename your downloaded files EXACTLY as:
   coin-flip.mp3
   coin-land.mp3

STEP 4: REPLACE FILES
---------------------
Replace the placeholder files in:
   assets/sounds/coin-flip.mp3
   assets/sounds/coin-land.mp3

STEP 5: RESTART APP
-------------------
- Stop your Expo dev server (Ctrl+C)
- Clear cache: npx expo start -c
- The sounds should now play!

QUICK TEST SOUNDS
-----------------
For quick testing, you can:
1. Record your own sounds on your phone
2. Convert them to MP3
3. Name them correctly and add to the folder
